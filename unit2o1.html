The Copyright (Computer Programs) Regulations 1992, a supplementary statute, extends rights to software usually protected by copyright-specific laws and agencies.
Works are protected upon development, ensuring you receive copyright protection as soon as you publish your website.

Individuals see the pages of a website in multiple viewports, that is, screens of different sizes.

Flexibly building. Account for the fact that your designs will be stretched and distorted by viewers.
For example, considering that text can expand when a user zooms in on your page, if you design with versatility, you can create page designs that bend but do not split when viewed in different environments.

1.4-Describe which are the appropriate file types for websites


After assessing the various types of files to facilitate an efficient website. I decided to pick three file types that are appropriate for new developer:
Html
Every few years, HTML is updated to make room for changes. To monitor how your pages are viewed and make pages more available, Cascading Stylesheets are used.
JavaScript, which adds a lot of power to basic HTML, offers basic special effects and interaction.
Much of this advanced stuff is down the road for later, but you have a lot of control at your disposal by using all of these technology together.
PNG/JPEG
Unlike JPEGs, PNGs also give you power over transparency, making them pretty much ideal for logos.
Lossless compression is often used for PNGs, meaning they do not sacrifice any data or consistency.
However, one of the main drawbacks of using PNGs is that they are not supported by some web browsers.its great for uploading pictures on your website
It simply won't show if your logo is saved as a PNG and someone is browsing your website on a browser that doesn't support them.
css
The traditional way to describe the presentation of your HTML pages, from fonts and colours to the full layout of a website, is now with cascading style sheets.
They are much more successful at describing the look of your website than using HTML on every page.
CSS is becoming an everyday language that is more important to know, so the faster you have a grasp on this most elegant of presentational languages, the better.
This tool can be beneficial when it comes to the background, font-text, and colour of all your webpages.
